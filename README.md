# Beep2Burn – Account Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your Beep2Burn account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

---

## How to Request Deletion

### Option 1 – In App

1. Open the **Beep2Burn** app  
2. Go to **Settings**  
3. Select **"Delete Account"**  
4. Confirm deletion  

### Option 2 – Email Request

If you cannot access the app, email:  
**mumscriptdev@gmail.com**

Include:

- Your registered email address  
- The subject line: **"Account Deletion Request"**

---

## What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon verification, we will delete:

- Your account information  
- Authentication credentials  
- Profile data  
- App usage data linked to your account  

---

## What Data May Be Retained

We may retain:

- Transaction records (if required by law)  
- Logs required for fraud prevention or security  

---

## Retention Period

Account data is deleted within **7 days** of verification.  
Some legally required records may be retained for up to **90 days**.

---

If you have questions, contact **mumscriptdev@gmail.com**
